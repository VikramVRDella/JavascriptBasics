Language Model
	The model that can be learned with large amount of data and uses many languages.
	Advanced AI Systems designed to understand and generate human language.

Features of LLM:
	Architecture : Based on neural network, particularly the Transformer Model
	Training : Trained on extensive datasets, learning grammar, context, and facts.

Capabilities of LLM:
	Text-Generation : Creates coherent and relevant text.
	Context : Recognizes nuances, idioms and cultural references.
	Conversational-Skills: Engages in natural, human like dialogs.
